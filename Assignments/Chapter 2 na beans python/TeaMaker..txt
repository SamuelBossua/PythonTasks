1. Algorithm for making a cup of tea (with decision + loop)

1. Fill a kettle with water.
2. Turn on the kettle and heat the water.
3. **Wait until the water boils** *(this is a loop — you keep waiting/checking until it boils)*.
4. Put a tea bag into a cup.
5. Pour the hot water into the cup.
6. Leave the tea to steep for a few minutes.
7. Remove the tea bag.
8. If you want sugar, then add sugar; otherwise, do nothing** *(this is the decision/if-else step)*.
9. Stir the tea.
10.Drink and enjoy.
