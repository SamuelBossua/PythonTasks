 2. Algorithm for crossing a road safely

1. Go to the edge of the road.
2. Look left, right, and left again.
3. If there are cars coming, then wait; otherwise, go to step 5** *(this is the conditional step)*.
4. Keep checking for traffic until no cars are coming** *(this is the loop)*.
5. Walk straight across the road.
6. Stay alert while crossing.
7. Reach the other side safely.
